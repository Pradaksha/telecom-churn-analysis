# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

This project analyzes customer churn in a telecom company using **PYTHON ,SQL,and Tableau**.  
The goal is to identify why customers leave and how it impacts revenue.

---

##  Business Problem
Telecom companies lose revenue when customers leave.  

- Who is churning?
- Why they are leaving?
- Which services and contracts cause high churn?
- How much revenue is being lost?

---

##  Dataset
The dataset contains **7,032 customers** with:
- Contract type  
- Internet service  
- Payment method  
- Monthly charges  
- Tenure  
- Churn status  

Source: Telco Customer Churn dataset

---

## KPIs Calculated using SQL
- Total Customers  
- Churned Customers  
- Churn Rate  
- Total Revenue  
- Revenue Lost  

SQL queries used are in **sql_queries.sql**

---

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month customers have the highest churn
- Fiber optic users churn more than DSL
- Electronic check users churn the most
- High churn leads to major revenue loss

---

##  Outcome
This dashboard helps business teams:
- Identify risky customers
- Reduce churn
- Protect revenue
