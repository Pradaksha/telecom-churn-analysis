##  Dashboard Insights

### 1. Churn by Contract
- Month-to-month customers have **~43% churn**
- Two-year contract customers have **~2% churn**
> Long-term contracts significantly reduce churn.

### 2. Churn by Internet Service
- **Fiber optic users churn the most**
- Customers without internet churn the least

### 3. Churn by Payment Method
- **Electronic check users churn the most**
- **Auto-payment users churn the least**

---

##  Business Recommendations
- Push customers toward **long-term contracts**
- Incentivize **auto-payment methods**
- Investigate service quality for **fiber optic customers**
